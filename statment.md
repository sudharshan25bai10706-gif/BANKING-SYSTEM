Problem Statement :

Customers need a reliable system to manage essential banking operations such as deposits, withdrawals, and balance inquiries.
Manual processes are time-consuming, error-prone, and lack proper record-keeping.
A computerized banking system is required to automate transactions, maintain accurate account data, and improve overall efficiency.
Scope of the project
The project will provide basic banking operations such as account creation, deposit, withdrawal, fund transfer, and balance inquiry.
It will store and manage customer account details securely with proper validation and error handling.
The system will generate transaction records and allow viewing of account history for better financial tracking.

Target users :
1.Bank Users
2.Retail Bank customer

High-level features :

Provides core banking operations such as account creation, deposits, withdrawals, fund transfers, and balance inquiries.
Maintains secure, accurate customer data with real-time transaction processing and record management.
Offers a simple, user-friendly interface for customers and administrators to perform banking activities efficiently.
